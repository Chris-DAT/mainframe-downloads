Official standards & guidelines

ibm.com/standards/web/design/video-looper/


What

A small widget/utility that will loop a (background) video the # of times you tell it to.


Why

Because you can't do it with standard html5 video element. It's either play once, or loop infinite. There is no native "loop x times" functionality.


Rules & guidelines

  • Required: Do not add the loop attribute on the <video> element.
  • Required: Add the autoplay attribute on the <video>.

How

  • Embed your video using the html5 <video> element.
  • Add the data-widget="videolooper" data-playtimes="3" attributes, setting the # of times to what you want.
