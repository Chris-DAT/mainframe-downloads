Official standards & guidelines

ibm.com/standards/web/design/data-tables/


What

The horizontal scroll feature of the data tables widget allows your table to expand beyond the size of the column it's in and scroll horizontally


Why

Sometimes you have a really wide table that just can't fit or look right when constrained to a given column width and it may be a better user experience to let the table scroll horizontally so the data can at least be read properly.


What's new

Slightly different than v17, but following v18 widget conventions, simply add the proper new data attributes to enable the table to have horizontal scroll


Rules & guidelines

Try to keep horizontal scrolling to a minimum.


How

  • Make a standard data table.
  • Add the data-widget="datatable" attribute to your <table> to enable the widget.
  • Add the data-scrollaxis="x" attribute to your <table> to enable horizontal scrolling.
